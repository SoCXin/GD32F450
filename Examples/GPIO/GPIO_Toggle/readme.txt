  @brief
    This demo is based on the GD32150R-EVAL/ GD32190R-EVAL board, on the GD32150R-EVAL,
  LED1 connected to PC10. LED2 connected to PC11. LED3 connected to PC12. LED4 connected
  to PD2. on the GD32190R-EVAL, LED1 connected to PA11.LED2 connected to PA12.LED3 connected 
  to PB6. LED4 connected to PB7. After system start-up, firstly, four LEDs on. then, 
  four LEDs can light cycles.
